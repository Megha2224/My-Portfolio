IOT Based Pollution Level Monitoring

IOT Based Pollution Monitoring System proposed for monitoring weather conditions like temperature, humidity, Gas , Ph Level using sensors and Raspberry pi . Sensors detect changes in environment and send it to the users for making analysis.

  System is used to monitor the Air Quality over a web server using Internet. It will trigger an alarm when the air quality goes down beyond
a certain level, means when there are sufficient amount of harmful gases present in the air like CO2, smoke, alcohol, benzene, NH3 and NOx.
